MSc Module Registration for the LSP

As many of you know we have several students delayed with visa problems. We will be using this system to provide details of our modules to these students so that they can register late.

The idea of this system is for you to provide material that students will be looking at across the first five weeks of the semester. Please be as detailed as you can about what you intend to cover across this time.

Please count how many documents you need to upload (documents such as course notes, tutorial sheets, past exam papers etc.) and provide them in the second box. If you need more documents than is allowed for in that box contact Neil.
