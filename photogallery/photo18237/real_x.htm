The Boiler House/Laundry Room

This outbuilding is thought to have been built at the same time as the main house, when it may have contained the boiler room for the central heating and a laundry room.

The billiards room/garage.

Not contempary with the main building, and converted to a Garage in the 1920s this building has a fireplace and a skylight.

The rear of the house.

The conservatory is a recent addition, designed by Mr Kingsland. The right hand part currently acts as a utility room.
